AboutUs

Astra health, being established in 2015 will rapidly emerge as one of the most technologically advanced healthcare and diagnostic centers. Head quartered in Hyderabad at Manikonda region and being the urban diagnostics is uniquely placed to become the premier diagnostic centre for doctors and patients.

Being founded by group of highly qualified entrepreneurial professionals including Doctors with diversified experience and also cross cultural exposure have designed an extraordinary and an ultramodern healthcare destination.

The company is one of the few healthcare and diagnostics which has on its panel a large team of fully qualified Pathologists, clinical Biochemists, MD Physicians, Gynaecologists, and Paediatricians and qualified Technologists and support staff who ensure quick turnaround time.

The company has been continuously investing in large clinic spaces, advanced diagnostic equipment’s sourced from world experts in their respective fields. Not to mention, our timely investments in advanced IT systems to facilitate across the board access of cases and facilities for online reporting.

The centre have well trained receptionists and managers who make it their mission in making patients feel comfortable and at ease at the reception desk.

Keeping in mind that in India quality healthcare services are still not affordable or either not reachable easily to many.

Astra Diagnostics continues to raise the bar to achieve optimum accuracy and precision, besides constantly endeavouring to propagate wellness and preventive healthcare through series of health check-up packages and patient care initiatives.
