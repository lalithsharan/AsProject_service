Pharmacy

Astra pharmacy is the health care service, which comprises the art, practice, and profession of choosing, storing, compounding, and dispensing medicines and advising healthcare professionals and patients on their safe, effective and efficient use Astra pharmacy is a specialized field of pharmacy which forms an integrated part of patient health care in a health facility.

Hospital pharmacy is the profession that strives to continuously maintain and improve the medication management and pharmaceutical care of patients to the highest standards in a comprehensive healthcare setting.

The missions of the pharmacist is to be part of the medication management in clinics, which encompasses the entire way in which medicines are selected, procured, delivered, prescribed, administered and reviewed to optimize the contribution that medicines make to producing informed and desired outcomes and to ensure the 7 “rights” are respected: right patient, right dose, right route, right time, right drug with the right information and documentation
